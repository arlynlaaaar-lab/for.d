Book in a Corner

I found you in a corner, not right nor left in sight,
but in the quiet middle where unnoticed things feel right.
A book I used to pass by, untouched, unread, unseen,
became the one I opened where my silence slipped between.
Each chapter was a footstep closer to your core,
yet every page I turned warned me of an ending at the door.

Still, every chapter felt eternal like time refused to end,
as if infinity was waiting in the margins we defend.
Though the final page draws nearer, I no longer feel the fear—
I’ll write the blank pages myself, keep your story here.
